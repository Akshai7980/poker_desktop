Note:

  • Day is treated as 12:00:00 AM - 11:59:59 PM.Week is treated as Sunday - Saturday.
  • Once the deposit limit (Amount or Count)is set, you cannot increase the limit (Amount or Count) for 7 days, except for reducing the limits.
  • When you set your deposit limit, any deposit you have made in the passed days of that week will count towards your new deposit limit.

Example: 8L now you cannot deposit more than Rs. 6L and you deposited Rs 5L and changed the deposit limit to Rs. 8 L now you cannot deposit more than Rs 3L till the week ends. Rs. 8L limit will be available from next week.
