Set custom Tournament limits based on the Buy-in price to participate in a Tournament.

Once set, the app will restrict you from playing Poker Tourneys on any table where the Buy-in fee is more than the limit set.

You can choose the “Play-All” option If you want access to all Tournaments on the App

If you are pre-registered for any real money tournaments that are higher than the Buy-in limit opted for,you will be unregistered from the same and the amount will be credited back to your Deposit wallet.

Running tournaments that you are registered for remain unaffected by these set limits.

If you win a free ticket from any satellite tourney and its related final tournament Buy-in is more than the limit set by you, we will restrict you from joining that tournament. As per company policy the value of the satellite ticket will be credited back in form on Instant Bonus to your wallet.

Note: Once Table limit is set, you cannot change them for 7 days, except for reducing the limits.
