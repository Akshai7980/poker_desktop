Set custom Self-Exclusion limit to block yourself from playing any game on Adda52 Poker. Once set, the App will restrict you from playing any Poker variant across the App, in addition, no cash deposits will be allowed during the period of time selected by you

You will however be allowed to log in the App, make withdrawals requests as applicable.

If you are pre-registered for any SnG or Cash tournaments, you will be unregistered from the same and the amount will be credited back to your Deposit wallet.

Running Cash games, SNG tournaments that you are registered for/playing remain unaffected by these set limits.

Note: Once the time period is set, you cannot change it back during that window.
